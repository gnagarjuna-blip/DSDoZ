Defining the Corporate Tone

A CX Manager is the guardian of the brand's voice. Unlike frontline agents who focus on resolution, a Manager ensures that the manner of resolution builds equity.

Core Principle: "We don't just solve problems; we restore confidence."

Effective corporate communication must be Concise, Courageous, and Consistent. Avoid "Hedge Words" like 'maybe', 'we'll try', or 'possibly'.
