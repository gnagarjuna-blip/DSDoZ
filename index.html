Module 1: Brand Excellence & Journey Mapping

Core Training for Customer Experience Team Managers

Day 1: The Brand Persona & Authority Week 1

1.1 Understanding the Corporate Voice

A Team Manager must embody the brand's persona. Our voice is defined as Empathetic Authority. This means we don't just solve problems; we reassure the user that they are in expert hands.

1.2 The "Active Voice" Mandate

Avoid passive language. Instead of "Your ticket was received," use "I have received your ticket and I am investigating the root cause." This establishes ownership.

Day 2: Mapping the Emotional Journey Week 1

2.1 Identifying Friction Points

The journey isn't a straight line. Managers must identify 'Pain Points'—specific moments where users feel confused or frustrated, such as the Onboarding or Billing phases.

2.2 Moments of Truth

These are the make-or-break interactions. When a system fails, the recovery process is more important than the failure itself.

Day 3: Quality Assurance (QA) Leadership Week 1

3.1 The 4-Pillar Rubric

Managers evaluate teams based on: Accuracy, Tone, Speed, and Resolution. A response can be fast but fail if the tone is robotic.

3.2 Calibrating Team Standards

Ensuring every agent provides a consistent experience. No "Hero Agents"—the system should produce excellent results regardless of who answers.

Day 4: Advanced De-escalation Week 1

4.1 The HEAT Technique

Hear the customer, Empathize, Apologize, and Take action. Managers step in when the HEAT technique is insufficient for an agent.
